Description

Zell am See a paradise for kids, with it´s open and wide slopes, lots of fun for kids, fun park, slalom courses magic carpets and forest runs. Outdo's childrens skischool is designed for children between the ages of 4-14. Our top priority is to ensure that your children have a wonderful holiday experience and learn to ski in a safe environment. To ensure that each child is well taken care of we aim for a maximum of 10 participants per group. 5 day ski course finishes with a treasure hunt which we believe is much fairer and more educational than a ski race. At the end of the treasure hunt all our kids are rewarded with a gift..

More questions?

Functional skiwear/equipment, lift pass, sunscreen, tissues and money for lunch.

Lift tickets are not included in the price of the lessons. Everyone accessing the mountain must have a valid lift ticket.

During the actual lesson time, we recommend that parents observe from a distance to prevent disrupting the class.
